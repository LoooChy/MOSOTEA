The Experience

Details about the atmosphere, what to expect, and the flow of our rituals.

How long is a session?

expand_more

Ceremonies typically range from 90 minutes to 3 hours, depending on the chosen workshop and the natural flow of the tea service. We encourage guests to arrive without a rigid schedule to fully immerse in the practice.

What's included in my booking?

expand_more

All premium Taiwanese teas, traditional seasonal tea snacks, and a guided narrative on tea history and preparation are included. You will also have access to our contemplative garden spaces before and after your session.

Do I need prior knowledge of tea?

expand_more

No prior knowledge is required. We welcome those at every stage of their tea journey, from curious beginners to seasoned connoisseurs. Each ceremony is tailored to the resonance of the participants.

Booking & Policies

Information on reservations, cancellations, and group sizes.

How do I manage my booking?

expand_more

Bookings are managed through our online portal. You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment. Cancellations made 48 hours in advance receive a full refund.

What are the group size limits?

expand_more

To maintain tranquility, our maximum group size is strictly limited to 6 guests. This ensures an intimate dialogue between the tea master and each participant.

Is there a weather policy?

expand_more

Outdoor tea tours may be rescheduled in heavy rain. Indoor ceremonies proceed regardless of weather; many find the sound of rain on our pavilion roof enhances the meditative experience.

Location & Access

Finding your way to our mountain sanctuary and accessibility details.

Arriving at the Site

Complimentary on-site parking is available. We suggest arriving 15 minutes early. The tea house is accessed via a short, gentle gravel pathway through the gardens.

Accessibility

While our main pavilion is accessible, some parts of the garden trails are uneven. Please contact us in advance if you have specific mobility requirements.

Still curious?

Our concierge is happy to assist with bespoke group bookings, dietary requirements, or private tea consultations.
