Moso Tea is more than a workshop; it is a family-run bridge between the lush high-mountain tea plantations of Taiwan and the serene lifestyle blocks of Lower Hutt.

Our mission is to preserve the authentic culture of tea—not just as a beverage, but as a medium for mindfulness and connection. We prioritize sustainability in every leaf we source and every practice we share.

Sustainability

Direct-trade, organic practices and zero-waste ceremonies.

Authenticity

Three generations of family knowledge in tea preparation.

Carefully curated experiences designed to lower your heart rate and sharpen your senses.
